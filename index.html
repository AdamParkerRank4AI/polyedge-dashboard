Three Types of Edge

1. Breaking News (mins-hours)
Wire services break it. Polymarket hasn't repriced. Trump/China had a 24-36hr window.
2. Mispricing (days)
Markets stay wrong. "Regime fall" at 10.8% ten days into war. Obvious to anyone watching.
3. Time Decay (predictable)
Deadline markets converge to 0/100%. If it's not happening, sell YES. Lowest risk.

How PolyEdge Works

Step 1: Scanner Finds an Edge

Every 15 mins: pulls 200 markets, checks 10 RSS feeds + 12 Bluesky news accounts. Matches headlines to markets. Score 5+ = email alert.

Step 2: You Decide

Is the news genuinely new? Not a rehash.
Should it move this market? Direct impact, not tangential.
Has the market already moved? Check the price. If it crashed, you're late.
Enough liquidity? Over $100K volume = safe.

Step 3: You Buy

Event MORE likely
Buy YES. E.g. "Visit by May 31?" — dates confirmed. Buy YES, sell when it rises.
Event LESS likely
Buy NO. E.g. "Ceasefire by Mar 31?" — active war. Buy NO, sell as it drops.

Step 4: Watch — Sell Signals

TAKE PROFIT — 15%+ in your favour. Market repriced. Sell.
TIME DECAY — Deadline within 3 days + profitable. Take the sure money.
STOP LOSS — 10%+ against you. You were wrong. Cut losses.
HOLD — Profitable but not at target. Wait.

Step 5: Sell

Place sell order on Polymarket. USDC back in wallet. Typical hold: 1 hour to 2 days. Target: 15-50% return. Fee: 2% of profit.
